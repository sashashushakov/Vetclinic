About Us

Why choose Highfield Vets?

We pride ourselves on being an understanding, friendly and approachable vet practice near you, that will take the time to explain anything you need to know about your pet’s treatment. We are a relatively small team at a single site in Sheffield, so it is usually possible to see the same vet or vet nurse, to allow continuity of care.

The practice has been established for over forty years. However, when it comes to the facilities we offer, we strive to offer the most up-to-date care and treatments.

In 2009, we opened our brand new sterile operating theatre with "gold standard" anaesthesia and state-of-the-art lighting.

In 2015, we had a major building renovation offering three new consulting rooms, a new lab with the most up-to-date equipment and a brand new x-ray suite fitted with digital radiography.

We are a Veterinary Nurse Training Centre, and pride ourselves on the standard of patient care our trainee and qualified nurses provide. 

In 2021 we completed the transformation, on what used to be our grooming parlour, into our brand new bespoke cat only waiting room and consultation rooms. This provides a safe, relaxing and ISFM cat friendly practice compliant area for our feline friends. We are very proud of our cat clinic and have a range of cat only consulting times throughout the week.

We're always striving to offer the best treatment and care for you and your pet.

How does it work?

With hectic schedules and Covid-19 restrictions, it’s not always ideal to pick up your pet’s supplies in practice. The My Family Vets online shop means you can stay stocked up without leaving the house, and your pet never has to go without their delicious diet and vet-approved products.

  • Browse our vast selection of diets and products, all approved by expert vets
  • Choose what you want and checkout online (redeeming your discount if you’re on our Pet Health Plan)
  • We can deliver to your door in just 3 working days*

Who are My Family Vets?

My Family Vets is a national network of locally run, independent-minded veterinary practices.

As a My Family Vets practice, we can offer you the best of both worlds: the wholesome service of a small business, plus the great value of a national brand.

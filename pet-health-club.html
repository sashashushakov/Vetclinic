What is Pet Health Club™?

Pet Health Club™ is the perfect companion for your pet, offering preventative healthcare for dogs, cats and rabbits, all for one low monthly payment. Spread the cost of vaccinations, parasite prevention and health checks and save £100’s each year.

Save £100s each year

Vet-prescribed treatments

Trusted by 600,000 owners

Why join?

 

  • Get complete peace of mind that your pet’s well looked after
  • Save up to £242 a year on your pet’s preventative healthcare
  • Vet-prescribed parasite treatment tailored to your pet's lifestyle
  • One monthly payment

What’s included in the plan?

Our routine healthcare plans are for dogs, cats and rabbits and include:

  • Annual vaccinations

  • Year-round flea, worm & tick treatment (dogs & cats)

  • Flystrike prevention (for rabbits)

  • Six-month health check

  • Microchipping or £10 voucher

  • 20% off neutering

Which plans do we offer?

Dog Health Care Plan £17.99 per month

Cat Health Care Plan £15.99 per month

Rabbit Health Care Plan £12.99 per month

Plus discounts on other services and products!
